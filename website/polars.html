Ever built the perfect house in Minecraft but wished you had done it in a different location? You can use coordinates and some of the block operations in the BLOCKS Toolbox drawer to copy and paste entire portions of the Minecraft landscape. You can also copy hillsides, lakes, and entire buildings. Changing the cordinates in the coding section allows for you to run the code, and the building appears without you building. In this project, you will use three different on chat command commands: "start" : This sets one corner of the area to be copied. "stop" : This sets the opposite corner of the area to be copied. "copy" : This creates an exact copy of everything between the start and stop points, placing it at your current position. ________________________________________________________
