Title: Lost in Time This is time game created using python. The user is sent from a scientist to travel through different places through a time machine. He goes to different places and ultimately faces challenged on the way like the time machine breaking. Almost getting eaten by a dino or even getting stuck in the future is the terrible things that happen to this player in this game. Even when you try to go home, you end up in an other world.Through help from someone in a different universe, he gets help to go back home.
